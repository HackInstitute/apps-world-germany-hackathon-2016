Hack @ Apps-World 2016

Apps World Germany is the main meeting place for the growing European developer community, new and exciting start-ups and the most innovative mobile first brands, to discuss the hottest and most current topics within the mobile and app space in the region.

Join our two day hackathon to work on new ideas and applications. We will provide you with food and drinks and there will be some entertainment as well.

Who can join the hackathon?

Everyone can join. We are especially looking for developers and designers with a passion for building products and working on new projects.

Should I register for the hackathon?

Yes, you absolutely should. It is a very unique type of event that brings together great people to work on new ideas.

What should I take with me?

You should bring your notebook and any additional hardware you need to work. We also recommend to take headphones.
